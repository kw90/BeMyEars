Be My Ears
Never get hit again by a car while listening to your favorite song

Problem

Thousands of good looking and amiable dudes and smart ladies get hit by cars every year in our streets when listinging to their favorite Céline Dion sound with their newest Apple earbuds miss aggressive cars when crossing the streets.

Apart from the aesthetic provocation of human blood in our boulevards we believe that it's a human right to get notified before dying.

Solution

That's why we developed Be My Ears.
Be My Ears wakes you up from your comatose love song vegetation by titillating your crown jewels when a car is on the verge of hitting you.

Be My Ears is an AI powered application for Android that uses the vibra alarm of your smartphone to notify you before the actual impact.
